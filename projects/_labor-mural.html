Explore Maine’s History of Labor Mural

2020, Maine State Museum

About this Interactive

The interactive kiosk in front of the mural in the lobby of the Maine State Museum.

Mural Panel with Hotspots

The visitor can swipe through the eleven panels; each panel has tappable hotspots that link to additional information.

Hotspot Popup

Each tappable hotspot leads to a popup window.

Introduction to this Panel

Each panel is interpreted with a brief introduction to the topic depicted in the panel and links to related material below.
