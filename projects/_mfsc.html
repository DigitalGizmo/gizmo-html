About this site

This website presents the Massachusetts Local Food Action Plan created by the Massachusetts Food System Collaborative. The goal of the Plan is to improve our local food system.

The Plan

MFSC's Plan addresses the opportunities and challenges of Massachusett's local food system and address equity, sustainability, access, production and distribution.

Search the Plan

The Plan is fully searchable using keywords as well as checkboxes to narrow results across multiple categories.
